# Product

## Register

product

## Users

WaxOnEdge is used by WAX blockchain traders who need to compare and execute swaps through a DEX aggregator. Users are likely working in a data-heavy trading context where clarity, speed, and trust matter more than decorative presentation.

## Product Purpose

WaxOnEdge aggregates swap routes across WAX liquidity sources so users can find reliable execution paths. Success means routes and market data are fast, correct, and easy to act on.

## Brand Personality

Precise, resilient, direct. The product should feel operational and trustworthy, with visual decisions supporting repeated use.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, decorative dashboards, noisy crypto hype visuals, and interface patterns that obscure trade state or route quality.

## Design Principles

- Performance is visible in the interface: keep surfaces fast to scan and quick to operate.
- Correctness outranks flourish: make important states, data, and failures explicit.
- Use compact hierarchy: prioritize route, price, liquidity, and status information over decoration.
- Preserve confidence: use restrained copy and predictable controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone for trading states.
